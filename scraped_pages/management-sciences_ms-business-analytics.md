As organizations increasingly recognize the importance of analytics in driving business success, SZABIST offers a forward-looking MS Business Analytics program tailored to equip students with the critical skills and insights needed for the future. With60%of organizations acknowledging the rising significance of analytics skills, our program integrates robust business acumen with cutting-edge analytics tools, emphasizing descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive analytics.

According to the Future of Jobs Report (2023) by the World Economic Forum, the demand for Business Analytics professionals is projected to grow by30-35%over the next four years. This surge is driven by advances in frontier technologies and the increasing adoption of big data. Industries such asFinancial Services (31%),Retail & Wholesale of Consumer Goods (37%), andSupply Chain & Transportation (42%)are projected to experience significant growth, making expertise in business analytics more valuable than ever. With analytics expected to create 1.4 million new jobs between2023and2027, MSBA graduates will be well-prepared to take on leadership roles across diverse industries, including finance, marketing, HRM, and project management.

At the core of MSBA program is a focus on leveraging AI for predictive analysis and optimization, ensuring that graduates are at the forefront of innovation in data-driven decision-making. The MSBA program also cultivates an entrepreneurial mindset, encouraging students to innovate and create value through data, while preparing them to tackle real-world challenges and lead new ventures. The opportunity to engage in hands-on research enable students to explore emerging trends and contribute to advancements in business analytics, with collaboration on projects enabling them to produce impactful findings that shape industry practices.

Supported by mentorship and access to top-tier resources, MSBA program ensures that students gain practical experience in research in business analytics, data management, analytics, and visualization techniques. This positions MSBA graduates as valuable assets in a data-centric world, where strategic decision-making is paramount.
